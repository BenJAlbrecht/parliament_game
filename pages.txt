PARLIAMENT GAME — SCREENS
=========================

Flow:

  [Page 1: Party Grid] --> [Page 2: Party Detail] --> [Page 3: Coalition Formation] --> [Page 4: Parliament Chamber]


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PAGE 1: Party Grid
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  HTML:  index.html  #screen-select > #party-grid
  JS:    js/select.js  (renderGrid)

  The opening screen. All parties are shown as clickable
  cards. Clicking a card advances to Page 2.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PAGE 2: Party Detail
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  HTML:  index.html  #screen-select > #party-detail
  JS:    js/select.js  (showDetail)

  Shown after clicking a party card on Page 1. Displays
  full party info (summary, history, seats) and
  a "Lead this party" confirm button. Back arrow returns
  to Page 1.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PAGE 3: Coalition Formation
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  HTML:  index.html  #screen-coalition
  JS:    js/coalition.js  (showCoalition)

  Shown after confirming a party on Page 2. Displays the
  player's pre-set coalition partner(s) with their stats
  and starting coalition loyalty (100%). Confirm advances
  to Page 4.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PAGE 4: Parliament Chamber
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  HTML:  index.html  #screen-parliament
  JS:    js/parliament.js  (entry point + button wiring)
         js/render.js      (SVG seats, legend, results, history)
         js/vote.js        (session state + vote logic)

  The main game screen. Player calls votes on bills; MPs
  vote strictly along party lines. Coalition loyalty
  (stored per partner in vote.js) will modify partner MP
  votes once modifiers are implemented.

  Support modules (no direct DOM ownership):
    js/data.js    -- PARTIES, BILLS, LAYOUT constants
    js/layout.js  -- seat distribution math
